What is microneedling?

Microneedling is the process of rapidly puncturing the skin with a sterilized microneedle. The needle causes microtears on your skin, and your body produces chemicals to heal itself. The process lasts about an hour

Why do I need microneedling?

The aim is to stimulate collagen and elastin production, which can clear up acne, wrinkles, sun damage, and other skin issues.

What are the downsides of microneedling?

Microneedling causes irritated, red skin for the first few hours after microneedling. The microtears are painful, but we provide numbing cream and other pain relief.
